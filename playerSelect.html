TRIVIA-O-WAR

Welcome players to Trivia-O-War! Get your mental weapons ready as you take each other on in intellectual battle!

How To Play

Player must go through a list of pre-written trivia questions selecting from the answers provided to move through

Each trivia answered correctly adds 2 points to your score, any trivia answered incorrectly removes 1 point from your score

Player must earn 10 points total in order to complete the game

Good luck and have fun!
