TRIVIA-O-WAR

Welcome players to Trivia-O-War! Get your mental weapons ready as you take each other on in intellectual battle!

How To Play

Players take turns answering trivia questions, racking up points as questions are answered correctly!

Each question correctly answered adds 10 points to your score and the turn is passed to your opponent!

If any question is answered incorrectly, 5 points are deducted from your score and your turn is passed to your opponent!

The first player to reach a score of 100 points is declared the winner!

The player set to start the game off is selected at random

Click "Player Selected" to randomly select the starting player!

Good luck and enjoy the game!
